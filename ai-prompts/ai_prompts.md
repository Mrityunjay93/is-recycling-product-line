# AI Prompts for Candidate Evaluation

## 1. Crisis Management
Prompt: "Evaluate the candidate [NAME] for crisis management in a recycling production line. Consider their [EXPERIENCE] years of experience and skills: [SKILLS]. Provide a score from 0-100 based on ability to handle equipment failures, supply shortages, or safety incidents. Rubric: 0-30 (poor), 31-60 (average), 61-100 (excellent). Explain reasoning."

## 2. Sustainability Knowledge
Prompt: "Assess [NAME]'s sustainability knowledge for managing a recycling plant. With [EXPERIENCE] years and skills [SKILLS], score 0-100 on understanding eco-friendly processes, waste reduction, and compliance. Rubric: Depth of knowledge (40%), Practical application (40%), Innovation (20%)."

## 3. Team Motivation
Prompt: "Rate [NAME] on team motivation skills for a production line role. Based on [EXPERIENCE] years and [SKILLS], score 0-100 for inspiring workers, resolving conflicts, and boosting productivity. Rubric: Leadership (50%), Communication (30%), Empathy (20%)."

Note: In the app, we use mock random scores instead of real AI calls for simplicity.
